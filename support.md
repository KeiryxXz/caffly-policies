## Subscriptions

Caffly AI requires an active subscription.

**Manage or cancel your subscription**

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone  
2. Tap your **Apple ID**  
3. Tap **Subscriptions**  
4. Select **Caffly AI**

**Restore a purchase**

Open the app, go to the paywall or Profile, and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

We cannot issue refunds directly. Refund requests must go through Apple.

## Apple Health

Caffly AI can read sleep data from Apple Health to show insights.

**To connect Health**

1. Open **Profile** in the app  
2. Turn on **Apple Health Sync**

**If sleep data is missing**

- Confirm Health permissions are enabled in **Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Caffly AI**
- Make sure your sleep data is recorded in the Health app or by your Apple Watch
- Toggle Apple Health Sync off and on again in Profile

## iCloud Sync

Drink logs may sync through your private iCloud account.

**If sync is not working**

- Sign in to iCloud on your device (**Settings → [your name]**)  
- Check that iCloud Drive is enabled  
- Make sure you have available iCloud storage  
- Force-quit and reopen the app

We cannot access your private iCloud data. Sync is handled entirely by Apple.

---

## Logging drinks

**Camera or photo logging not working**

- Allow Camera and Photos access in **Settings → Caffly AI**
- Use a clear photo of the drink, can, or label
- If recognition fails, type the drink manually instead

**AI features require an internet connection.**

---

## Delete your data

You can remove all app data from **Profile → Privacy & Data → Delete All My Data**.

This removes local data and synced iCloud records associated with the app, subject to Apple's sync timing.
